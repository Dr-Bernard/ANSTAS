About TAS

TAS was deployed by CRESTSAGE LIMITED for the Anambra State Government on Monday 2nd of October 2023. This is targetted at discovering Civil Servants who are always at their duty posts to reward them accordingly.

The system aims to streamline and automate the process of employee attendance tracking, ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and transparency

Time to Clock in or out

When can I clock in or out?

Monday to Friday:

Monday to Friday:
Clock out by 3:00pm to 5:00pm daily.
