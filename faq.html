Here are Answers to some of the Frequently Asked Questions about the TAS:

  4. State Auditor General's office Building.

  8. Ministry of Women Affairs Building.
  9. Ministry of Power and Water Resources Building.
  10. Anambra State Physical Planning Board Headquarters Complex.


When can I clock in or out?

Monday to Friday:
Clock in by 7:00am to 9:00am daily.
Monday to Friday:
Clock out by 3:00pm to 5:00pm daily.


When can I start clocking in and out?

As soon as you are registered on the Biometric device you can start clocking in or out immediately.


How do I clock in and out?

Place your grease-free thumb on the Biometric Device while standing upright and it will capture you immediately.


How long does it take to clock in or out?

Once you place your thumb on the Biometric device, it captures you in less a second.


Are there other methods I can use to clock in or out?

The solution offers multiple methods for capturing your attendance, including biometric scanners, swipe cards, and manual entry.


What if I go on my annual leave?

The solution supports the tracking of leaves, vacations, and other absences, with appropriate approval workflows.


Will there be a training session to teach everyone how to use the solution?

The system is highly user-friendly, however, there will still be a training session for the employees at the commencement of operations of the solution.


As a Director / Head of Admin, can I view the attendance performance of the staff in my MDA?

The system provides real-time monitoring of attendance data, allowing supervisors to view employee attendance status and address any discrepancies promptly.
